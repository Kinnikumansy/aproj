What Is a Home Warranty?

A warranty for your home is an annual renewable service contract that provides protection and peace of mind, offering a safeguard for when certain essential household systems and appliances break. Home warranties help cut the high cost to repair or replace many of the systems and appliances in your home. They complement your homeowners' insurance by protecting things that aren’t covered under the umbrella of home insurance.

An American Home Shield home service plan is like a traditional home warranty but better, thanks to its extended coverage and exclusive member perks. The first layer of protection is to help with the cost and hassle that comes with repairing or replacing parts of home appliances and systems that break down over time. But we don’t stop there – our home service plans provide American Home Shield members with exclusive pricing on home maintenance services, new appliances, and a variety of home services. From tech installation and setup to pre-season HVAC tune-ups, we’ve got your back.

Why Do I Need One?

The short answer is that home warranty coverage is a proactive way to shield the home systems and appliances you rely on every day. It’s a cover-all approach that keeps you protected from the inconvenience of going without the essentials. When an item breaks down due to normal wear and tear, we will help with the cost to repair it. If it can't be repaired, we will replace the item for you (subject to the limitations and exclusions of your plan). It’s not just about the immediate benefits, either – a home service plan helps keep your budget under control and saves you time searching for a qualified contractor.

What's Covered?

American Home Shield home warranties, which we refer to as home service plans, protect the home systems and appliances that you use every day – no matter their age. You can shield the items that keep your home running smoothly and choose a plan that’s right for you. Our home service plans include covered repairs for major components of your heating and cooling systems (with our air conditioner warranty included in all plans), electrical systems, plumbing systems, kitchen appliances, and laundry appliances, as well as roof leak repair and more. Feel empowered to ditch that single-appliance extended warranty – American Home Shield offers you more protection and better value.

Are They Worth It?

Let’s provide a real-world example of how our home service plans can help you save money. The cost to repair or replace an A/C unit without a warranty can be up to $3,800* – you would be out of pocket for the total amount. In contrast, all three of our home service plans include A/C unit protection. If a breakdown occurs, you simply pay the Trade Service Call Fee you selected ($75, $100 or $150) when you purchased your plan to have it repaired or replaced (subject to the limitations and exclusions in your plan).

When you think about all of the home systems and appliances you use every day, repair and replacement costs can add up quickly. An American Home Shield home service plan is a great way to protect your budget and keep your home running smoothly. You can compare our home warranty plans to make sure you're choosing the coverage limits that make the most sense for your personal situation.


*Repair/replacement costs are the eightieth percentile of U.S. costs according to a nationwide survey of homeowners conducted in 2019 by ClearVantage for American Home Shield.
